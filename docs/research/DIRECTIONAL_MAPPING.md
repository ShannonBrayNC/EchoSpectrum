# Directional RF Mapping

EchoSpectrum supports lawful receive-only directional RF mapping research.

## Goals

- directional signal awareness
- spectrum heatmaps
- LPDA antenna studies
- GPS-tagged observations
- replayable field sessions
- civic resilience research

## Mapping Workflow

1. configure receive-only sensor profile
2. record directional observations
3. tag GPS coordinates
4. export GeoJSON sessions
5. visualize observations in mapping tools

## Observation Metadata

Each observation includes:
- latitude
- longitude
- antenna bearing
- signal strength estimate
- center frequency
- timestamp

## GeoJSON Export

EchoSpectrum exports GeoJSON-compatible datasets for:
- QGIS
- Leaflet
- Mapbox
- custom observatory dashboards

## Governance Boundaries

EchoSpectrum directional mapping:
- must not perform surveillance
- must not attribute signals to individuals
- must not interfere with communications
- must remain receive-only
