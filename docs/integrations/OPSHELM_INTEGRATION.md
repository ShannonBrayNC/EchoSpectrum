# OpsHelm Integration

EchoSpectrum integrates with OpsHelm to provide lawful RF observation summaries and operational telemetry exports.

## Goals

- operational awareness
- infrastructure telemetry enrichment
- incident export support
- governance-aware reporting
- replayable observation summaries

## Export Model

EchoSpectrum exports:
- RF finding summaries
- metadata-only observations
- governance mode indicators
- signal classification summaries
- directional mapping references

## Governance Requirements

Exports must:
- remain receive-only
- avoid private communication content
- avoid interception workflows
- preserve ETS governance metadata
- maintain auditability

## Future Enhancements

- replay-linked incidents
- SignalForge telemetry routing
- AI-assisted RF incident summaries
- customer-facing reporting templates
- distributed sensor correlation
