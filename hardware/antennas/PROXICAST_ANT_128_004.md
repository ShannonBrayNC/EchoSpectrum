# Proxicast ANT-128-004 LPDA Research Guide

## Hardware

- Proxicast ANT-128-004
- 600-6000 MHz
- 9-11 dBi LPDA directional antenna

## Intended EchoSpectrum Usage

- receive-only directional observations
- spectrum research
- ISM mapping studies
- ADS-B directional receive studies
- civic resilience observation

## Safety Requirements

EchoSpectrum usage must remain:
- receive-only
- non-interfering
- governance-aware
- compliant with ETS policy requirements

## Mounting Guidance

- maintain stable mast orientation
- minimize coax length
- use low-loss cable
- keep antenna polarity documented

## Observation Logging

Each field observation should include:
- GPS coordinates
- timestamp
- center frequency
- RSSI estimate
- antenna bearing
- environment notes
- weather conditions

## Suggested Research Bands

| Band | Purpose |
|---|---|
| 902-928 MHz | ISM studies |
| 2.4 GHz | Wi-Fi/BLE density |
| 5.8 GHz | Wi-Fi occupancy |

## Important Boundary

No transmit workflows should be connected to this antenna within EchoSpectrum operations.
